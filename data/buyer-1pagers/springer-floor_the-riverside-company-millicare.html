Buyer Profile

The Riverside Company is a global private equity firm founded in 1988 with over $14 billion in assets under management, headquartered in New York with offices in Cleveland, Dallas, San Francisco, and across Europe and Asia-Pacific. Riverside specializes in acquiring and growing companies valued at up to $400 million, with a particular strength in the lower middle market — businesses between $10M and $100M in enterprise value. Their operating philosophy centers on 'buy and build': acquire a strong platform company, then aggressively add bolt-on acquisitions to expand geographic reach, service lines, and customer density. Riverside has completed over 1,000 investments since inception, making them one of the most active PE firms globally by deal count. milliCare Floor & Textile Care was acquired by Riverside as a strategic add-on to their Clintar Commercial Outdoor Services platform, reflecting Riverside's thesis around building national commercial facility services networks through franchise and branch expansion. milliCare operates 40+ franchise locations across North America providing commercial carpet cleaning, hard floor care, textile restoration, and facility maintenance to corporate offices, healthcare facilities, schools, and government buildings. The company generates approximately $25 million in systemwide revenue and has been actively filling geographic gaps in its franchise map since the Riverside acquisition. Post-acquisition, Riverside typically installs professional management, upgrades technology and CRM systems, standardizes operating procedures across locations, and pursues an aggressive add-on acquisition cadence — often completing 3-5 bolt-ons per platform per year. Riverside's investment in milliCare signals a clear conviction that commercial floor care is a fragmented, recession-resistant vertical ripe for consolidation. The business model benefits from recurring revenue through maintenance contracts, low capital intensity, and high customer retention. Riverside's playbook here is straightforward: acquire independent operators in underserved territories, convert them to the milliCare brand and operating system, and drive margin improvement through purchasing power, shared back-office, and cross-selling across the Clintar/milliCare portfolio.

Springer's established local relationships and reputation give milliCare instant credibility in a market that would take years to build organically.
