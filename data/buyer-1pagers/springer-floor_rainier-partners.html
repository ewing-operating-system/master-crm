Why This Seller

Springer Floor fills a specific capability gap in Rainier's commercial cleaning platform. Kleen-Tech handles general janitorial — the bread-and-butter recurring contract work — but specialized floor care and carpet cleaning is a distinct skill set that commercial clients typically source separately. By acquiring Springer, Rainier can offer floor care as a bundled service to Kleen-Tech's existing accounts across 30 states, increasing revenue per account while reducing client churn. Springer's Des Moines base also gives Rainier a stronger Midwest presence, and at an estimated $900K in revenue, the company is sized right for a tuck-in that doesn't overwhelm integration capacity. The specialized nature of floor care also commands higher margins than general janitorial, improving the blended economics of the platform.
