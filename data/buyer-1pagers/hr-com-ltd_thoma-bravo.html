Thoma Bravo's playbook is well-documented: acquire a platform leader, bolt on complementary assets that deepen the moat, optimize margins, and either take public or sell to a strategic at a significant premium. They have executed this playbook dozens of times across cybersecurity, fintech, healthcare IT, and now HR tech.

For a seller evaluating Thoma Bravo as a potential partner, the firm brings unmatched resources, operational expertise, and a genuine track record of growing the businesses they acquire — not just stripping them for parts. This is a buyer that understands technology businesses at a fundamental level and has the capital to move decisively.
