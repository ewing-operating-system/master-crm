Why This Seller

Design Precast & Pipe fills the most obvious remaining gap in NWPX's national footprint: the Gulf Coast. With Geneva Pipe covering the Pacific Northwest, ParkUSA covering Texas, and Boughton's covering the Midwest, NWPX has no manufacturing presence along the Gulf Coast corridor — Mississippi, Louisiana, Alabama. Design Precast's Gulfport, Mississippi operation delivers exactly that: established precast concrete and pipe manufacturing capacity, a loyal regional customer base, and proximity to Gulf Coast infrastructure spending on ports, highways, and commercial development. At an estimated $15M in revenue, it's a clean bolt-on that fits NWPX's proven playbook of acquiring profitable, founder-led regional manufacturers and integrating them into the national platform without disruption.
